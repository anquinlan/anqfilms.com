About me

I'm An and I'm a

I first picked up a camera at the age eleven with the willingness to take pictures for my brother's track event that was held in Florida. It just first clicked to me, without the help of anyone, I knew instantaneously that this was a hobby that I was going to invest the majority of my childhood time in. Little did I know that it would then develop to where it is now. Later on, throughout the years, I would watch numerous tutorials going over transitions and teaching myself the many wonders of the realm of post-editing. I would find myself then taking Sam Kolder's Masterclass that I realized there was so much more that I needed to learn despite the many years of experience I had when it comes to filmmaking and photography. So over diligent studies in the technicalities, proper equipment, and shooting dynamics, I am proud to now utilize my skills to create awareness of the things going on in the world. As well as creating reliable marketing assets for several different corporations, organizations, and ambassadors.
